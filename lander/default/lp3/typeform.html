FAQs

How does pricing work?

The cost of an Audience Panel project is determined by taking the price per response multiplied by the number of responses. Price per response is based on i) the length of your survey in terms of question count and estimated minutes to complete and ii) the specificity of your audience. Centiment does not charge any project management fees and there is no sales tax in the United States. To determine the price for your target audience, request a quote.

Do I need a subscription?

Targeting survey respondents from Centiment’s Audience Panel does not require a subscription. Each project is priced individually without the need for any long-term commitments. Our Survey Tool is also offered free of charge when purchasing responses. If you’d like to gather responses from your own list of contacts, we do offer paid subscriptions to our Survey Tool.

Where can I build my survey?

Centiment offers an easy-to-use Survey Tool that can be used at no charge when you are purchasing targeted survey responses. Have a tool you already love? Centiment’s Audience Panel is compatible with all major tools including Qualticrics, SurveyMonkey, Alchemer, and more.

How does Centiment find respondents?

We pre-recruit and profile our panelists ahead of time. Upon signing up to answer surveys in exchange for a reward, panelists are put through a series of security checks and we begin to build out their targeting profiles. We monitor engagement utilizing a quality score metric to ensure respondents are consistently providing quality feedback. To protect the privacy of our participants, we do report any personally identifiable profile data.

Can Centiment target B2B respondents?

We can target both consumer and B2B survey respondents utlizing over 1,000+ demographic and firmographic data points. This includes but is not limited to age, gender, household income, employment status, industry, department, seniority, and decision-making authority.

Can Centiment reach international respondents?

We can reach respondents in 100+ countries. Please note you’ll often have to survey respondents in their local language. We off translation services and our Survey Tool can handle multi-language surveys.
