Here at Centiment, your deadlines are our deadlines. While fielding time is impacted by response count and targeting, most consumer-based surveys take just 1-3 days after the soft launch is completed. Highly targeted B2B surveys average 4-5 days.

How does Centiment ensure the accuracy of responses?

We maintain fraud scores on each respondent on our survey panel. This scoring is driven by behavioral patterns including time spent in surveys, inconsistent profile data reporting, and human-reviewed responses that have been flagged as low quality. If a respondent’s score dips below the acceptable threshold, they are banned from the platform.
