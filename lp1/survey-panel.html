What's the turnaround time?

Here at Centiment, your deadlines are our deadlines. While fielding time is impacted by response count and targeting, most consumer-based surveys take just 2-3 days after the Soft Launch is completed. B2B surveys average 4-5 days.

How does Centiment ensure the accuracy of responses?

We use a technology called fingerprinting that combines IP address, device type, screen size, and cookies to ensure only unique panelists enter your survey. 
‍
We also maintain fraud scores on respondents. This fraud scoring is driven by behavioral patterns including time spent in surveys, inconsistent profile data reporting, and responses that have been reviewed and flagged as low quality. Once a respondent breaches a certain fraud score, they are banned from the platform. Respondents are only compensated for good quality, completed surveys which translates into high-quality data for you!

Where does Centiment find survey respondents?

Our number one recruiting source is social media. Survey respondents volunteer their opinions and feedback in exchange for a reward -- but they’re only rewarded when they provide good quality data that meets your researching needs!

Rewards come in the form for PayPal cashouts or respondents can pledge their earnings to the nonprofit of their choice.
